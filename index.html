AI Match Constraints & Rules

Rule-Based Pre-Filters

  • Work Authorization Check: Hard pre-filter matches candidate nationality and authorization categories.
  • Visa Sponsorship Check: If visa sponsorship is not offered but required, application is automatically blocked.
  • Seniority Matching: JUNIOR roles restrict candidate experience up to 8 years; SENIOR roles require at least 3 years.
  • Location Pref Match: Automatically verifies onsite, hybrid, and remote preferences against job locations.

Semantic Vector Similarity

The matching score is computed dynamically out of 100 points, consisting of:

  • 🔥 Vector Cosine Distance (50%): Evaluated using Gemini text embeddings of job descriptions against your resume bullet points.
  • 🎯 Keyword Overlap (50%): Direct intersection of technical skill tags extracted from the job description and your facts profile.
